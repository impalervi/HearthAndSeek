v1.4.1

New Features
- Tooltip 3D preview — hovering any decor item (vendors, bags, crafting orders) shows a rotating 3D model beside the tooltip
- Crafting order overlay — decor ownership icons now appear on crafting order items, matching the vendor overlay
- New "Show 3D model on tooltip" toggle in Settings under the Tooltip section

Improvements
- Vendor overlay upgraded to three tiers: owned, uncollected with first-acquisition bonus, uncollected without bonus
- Settings header renamed from "Vendor & Crafting Order Overlays" to "Vendor & Crafting Overlays"

Bug Fixes
- Fixed tooltip preview failing after combat or loading screens due to stale ModelScene actors
- Fixed taint errors ("secret number") when tooltip preview anchored to secure GameTooltip frame
- Fixed tooltip preview positioning on non-default UI scale configurations
- Fixed detail panel and big viewer 3D models occasionally failing after instance transitions
